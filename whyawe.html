Meet the founder

Elvis Warutumo Gitau
Founder & Lead Engineer

Building the web agency
Africa deserves

Africa Web Experts was founded by Elvis Warutumo Gitau in Nairobi with a simple conviction: businesses in Africa and across the world deserve access to world-class web development — fast, affordable, and without the opacity that defines the traditional agency model.

Elvis saw firsthand how AI-powered development tools were transforming what a small, focused team could accomplish. Instead of hiring a hundred developers and charging enterprise rates, he built AWE around AI-native workflows that let a lean team deliver production-grade applications in a fraction of the time and cost.

Today, AWE serves clients across Kenya, Africa, and globally — proving that the best web applications do not need to come from the most expensive agencies or the largest teams. They need to come from the smartest workflows and the most committed people.

Our philosophy

How we think about
building for the web

The technical beliefs and operational principles that drive every project we take on.

01

Modern stack, proven technology

We use React, Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Supabase, PostgreSQL, and Vercel — the same tools trusted by the world's best engineering teams. No experimental frameworks, no proprietary lock-in.

02

AI is a multiplier, not a replacement

AI tools generate code, designs, and tests at machine speed. Human engineers provide the judgment, architecture decisions, and quality assurance that machines cannot. The combination is more powerful than either alone.

03

Performance is a feature

Every application we ship targets 95+ Lighthouse scores, sub-second load times, and excellent Core Web Vitals. Slow websites lose users and search rankings — we build fast from the start.

04

SEO is architecture, not an afterthought

Search engine optimization is built into the foundation — semantic HTML, structured data, meta configuration, sitemap generation, and server-side rendering are standard on every project, not add-on services.

05

Ship early, iterate often

We believe in getting a working product into real users' hands as fast as possible. Real feedback from real usage is more valuable than months of planning in isolation. We ship, we measure, we improve.
